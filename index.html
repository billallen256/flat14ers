Flat14ers

The best view of the mountains is not always from the mountains.

Mount Yale

  • Start at the bridge near the Barr Lake Visitor Center, 13401 Picadilly Road, Brighton, CO 80603
  • Walk around Barr Lake
  • Total distance of 9 miles (14.5 km), equivalent to hiking Mount Yale

Mount Massive

  • Start at Iron Horse Elementary, 20151 Tallman Drive, Parker, CO 80138
  • Walk between Iron Horse Elementary and Cimarron Middle School six times
  • Total distance of 14.5 miles (23.3 km), equivalent to hiking Mount Massive

Mount Bierstadt

  • Start at Prarie Crossing Elementary, 11682 Bradbury Ranch Road, Parker, CO 80134
  • Walk around school block nine times
  • Total distance of 7.5 miles (12 km), equivalent to hiking Mount Bierstadt

Torreys Peak

  • Start at Pioneer Elementary, 10881 Riva Ridge Road, Parker, CO 80138
  • Walk 2 miles (3.2 km) east and back along Sulpher Gulch Trail four times
  • Total distance of 8 miles (12.8 km), equivalent to hiking Torreys Peak
