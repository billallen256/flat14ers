The best view of the mountains is not always from the mountains.

Mount Yale

  • Start at the bridge near the Barr Lake Visitor Center, 13401 Picadilly Road, Brighton, CO 80603
  • Walk 9 miles (14.5 km) around Barr Lake is equivalent to hiking Mount Yale

Mount Massive

  • Start at Iron Horse Elementary, 20151 Tallman Drive, Parker, CO 80138
  • Walk six laps along Tallman Gulch Trail to Cimarron Middle School is equivalent to hiking Mount Massive

Mount Bierstadt

  • Start at Prarie Crossing Elementary, 11682 Bradbury Ranch Road, Parker, CO 80134
  • Walk around school block and back nine times is equivalent to hiking Mount Bierstadt

Torreys Peak

  • Start at Pioneer Elementary, 10881 Riva Ridge Road, Parker, CO 80138
  • Walk east to turnaround sign and return four times

Supplies

  • Sunscreen
  • Hat
  • First Aid Kit
  • Walking poles
